About Me

I hold a Bachelor of Music in Piano performance from the Univerisity of St Thomas.

I taught piano lesson for four years, both keys and music theory. There is something magical in watching different ages and personalities learn and create music.

I've also been a barista, a path paved by many others with artistic majors. Its chill, but past the latte art and dialing shots of espresso, there is a flow I love getting into to see how many microseconds I can take off my latte time.

When I'm not playing piano and writing music, I enjoy reading, doing yoga, and embroidering

But playing on my switch or watching a movie and cuddling up with my fiance and rabbit is just as therapudic
